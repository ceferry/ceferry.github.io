Welcome to Place de Poisson, where culinary traditions from both sides of the Pyrenees Mountains come together in a celebration of flavor and freshness. At our restaurant, we offer a curated selection of the finest tinned fish and seafood, sourced ethically, and expertly prepared to showcase their unique taste and texture.

Our menu is a tribute to the diversity and richness of the sea, with a perfect cocktail to pair with each dish.

Come and join us from 5 to 8pm every day for Apéro Hour for an unforgettable dining experience, where every dish tells a story of passion and tradition.
